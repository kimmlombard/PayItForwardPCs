Our Mission

The mission of Pay It Forward PCs is to enhance quality of life by providing free, high-quality personal computers to disadvantaged groups and individuals. We believe digital access is a fundamental necessity, not a luxury.

Quality You Can Trust

We do not just recycle old scrap. Our computers are sourced from trusted partners, including Amazon Renewed and Microsoft Authorized Refurbishers. Every system we donate comes with a warranty and return policy, ensuring our recipients receive reliable tools for work, school, and connection.
