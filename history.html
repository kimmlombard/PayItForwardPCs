Our History

The journey of Pay It Forward PCs has been one of persistence and dedication to doing things the right way.

The Beginning

We initially began our operations organized as an LLC. However, realizing that our mission was truly charitable, we embarked on the complex process of converting to a full non-profit entity to better serve our community.

Becoming a 501(c)(3)

Working closely with BryteBridge Consulting, we navigated the dissolution of our LLC status and filed for federal non-profit recognition. This transition was critical to ensuring transparency and maximizing the impact of every donation.
