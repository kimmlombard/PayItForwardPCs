Who We Are

We are a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the digital divide. By matching charitable contributions with specific needs, we put technology into the hands of those who need it most.

Who We Serve

Our efforts have supported a diverse range of individuals and organizations, including:

  • Disaster Relief: Victims of the Maui fires and the Maui YMCA.
  • Community Support: The United Way and Women's Recovery Center.
  • Local Aid: Teens in our local community and churches across Northern California and Maui.
